Integrate Self Sovereign Identity issuers and verifiers in minutes

Altme is a next-generation platform to integrate Decentralized Identity solutions in an application.

To use Altme, you don't need to learn Verifiable Credentials standards, DIDs signature or how to work with a complex SDK.

Instead, Altme uses WalletConnect, Beacon or OpenID as a simple, familiar method for building your application - you can now launch your SSI project in hours, not weeks.

On-chain and off-chain data check triggered by Verifiable Credentials

In some situations an application only needs an API to verify credentials, in other situations decentralized applications (dApp) need to have on-chain information. Several technologies are possible for this (SBT, Whitelist,...).

For us it is mainly a question of integrating ourselves with the architectures of our customers.
