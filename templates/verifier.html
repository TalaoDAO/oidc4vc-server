Integrate a SSI Verifier in minutes

The Verifiable Credentials exchange protocols between Issuers, Holders and Verifiers are relatively complex and not always stabilized. Credential design and signatures checks are also complex tasks. The result of all this is that the integration of a SSI Verifier or a SSI Issuer with a development sdk is generally a nightmare for application developers and hindrance to the deployment of SSI solutions.

Altme has set up a Saas-type platform that allows a company to configure its Verifier in less than 5 minutes and install it in less than an hour in any application developed with a traditional framework (Node, Python, Ruby, C, ...) or a CMS (Wordpress, Webflow,...).

Integrate a SSI Verifier in Web3 applications

In addition, Web3 integration requires in particular integration with crypto protocols, for this we have also developed gateways with Beacon for Tezos and WalletConnect for EVMs. Installing a Verifier which is activated during synchronization between your wallet and your dApp at the same time as the crypto transaction is now achievable in a few hours.

On-chain data check triggered by Verifiable Credentials

In some situations a dApp needs to have on-chain information. Several technologies are possible for this (SBT, Whitelist,...). For us it is mainly a question of integrating ourselves with the architectures of our partners such as Compellio or TezID.
