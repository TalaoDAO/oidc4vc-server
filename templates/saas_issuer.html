How it works ?


When the user scans the QR code, the third-party application receives a call on the “credential” webhook with the user's credentials in the form of a json web token.
The application produces the attestation and the transfer in response to the API. Talao's back end signs the certificate and transfers it to the user's wallet.
The credential is stored on the wallet.
