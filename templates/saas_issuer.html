How it works ?


When you want the user to collect her credential you redirect her browser to the issuer page provided by the platform so she can scan a QR code (or activate a deeplink).
Then your application receives a call on its webhook endpoint with eventually the requested credential of your choice in the form of a json.
Your application sends back either the data to include in a credential or the signed credential to transfer to the wallet.
If needed the platform signs the credential and transfers it to the user's wallet.
If the transfer is successful the user is redirected to your application callback endpoint.
Most of the parameters (DID method, keys, credentials, page design,...) can be configured on the platform in a couple of minutes.
