Issuers for companies

Company Pass
This pass is issued by a company or by Altme on behalf of the company. This pass maybe used for authentication for online services (Single Sign On) or even as a badge to enter company offices. Example : Arago cultural pass issuer

Free
Custom issuer : 2,500€, subscription fees 149€/month
Certificate of employment
This certificate is issued by a company or by Altme on behalf of the company. Data are stored in the user wallet only.

Setup fees : 2,500€, subscription fees 149€/month

Issuers of educational credentials

Student card
User can get that credential through a portal or with another mean of authentication (email, kyc,...). This credential is signed by a university, a school or a training center. No data are stored by Altme.

Setup fees : 2,500€, subscription fees 149€/month
Diploma
User can get that credential through a portal or a custom website. This credential is signed by the university and transfered to the wallet by Altme. The credential is compliant with the EU EBSI project. Diploma data are stored in the non custodial user wallet.

Setup fees : 2,500€, subscription fees 149€/month
